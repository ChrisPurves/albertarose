Helen Khan's Biography

Ms. Khan has over 30 years of experience in the Post-Secondary Education sector in Canada. She spent 27 years working for the University of Alberta before moving to Pakistan, where she now provides information about and services on behalf of UAlberta and Western.

University of Alberta

The University of Alberta is ranked in the top 100 of the world. The QS 2018 rankings hold UAlberta in 90th position. This research intense university ranks 5th in Canada and has 18 faculties with over 200 undergraduate programs and over 500 postgraduate programs. It offers many scholarships to international students.

Western University

Western is located in London, Ontario just 2 hours from Toronto. This research university is positioned 9th in Canada, and its clinical programs rank in the world’s top 100. Western’s prestigious Richard Ivey Business School is considered the best outside of the USA according to the Bloomberg Businessweek’s ranking and has been placed 66th by the Economist.

Alberta Rose Education Centre

Times change and so has Alberta Rose Education Centre. Our changes reflect changing demands in the marketplace. Alberta Rose Education Centre was established in April 2011 to meet the educational needs of students in Lahore, Pakistan and district. As the institution expanded, it started to meet the needs of Pakistani students, both nationally and internationally. Today, it is the office location for Ms. Helen Khan, representative of two pristine Canadian universities, the University of Alberta and Western University in Ontario, Canada.

If you are interested in a world-class education, think Canada. You will want to meet Ms. Khan. All her services are free of charge to students and schools wanting to learn more about UAlberta and Western.
