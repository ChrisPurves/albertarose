Alberta Rose

Alberta Rose Education Centre was established in April 2011 to meet the educational needs of students in Lahore, Pakistan and district. As the institution expanded, it started to meet the needs of Pakistani students, both nationally and internationally.

Today, Alberta Rose Education Centre has become a reputable institution that focuses on grooming its clients for an international market.
